Māmane
Hāpu'u

"Type" is hyphenated if, and only if, it's followed by a noun. So, "Pikachu is an Electric type. His Lightning Speed is a Normal-type move. This move is not very effective against Rock-type Pokémon."
Use commas when writing out numbers with four or more digits a la the move 100,000 Volts.
"Goodbye," not "Good bye."
The words "sir" and "ma'am" should be lower-case unless they're part of a title, or part of the address at the start of a letter.
